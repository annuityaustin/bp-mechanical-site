Who We Are

A mechanical partner contractors trust to get it right.

BP Mechanical, LLC provides commercial and industrial mechanical contracting throughout the Phoenix valley. From new construction and retrofit to ongoing service, our team handles projects of every size with the same priority: do it correctly, do it safely, and stand behind the work.

As a woman-owned business, we bring a hands-on, accountable approach that general contractors and facility owners count on — clear communication, dependable scheduling, and craftsmanship that holds up long after the job closes out.

Our factory-trained and certified technicians work across HVAC systems and process piping, backed by preventive maintenance programs that keep critical equipment running year-round.
